Deep Learning on Blockchain

Kynai democratizes Deep Learning by rewarding blockchain miners and users for contributing their CPU/GPU computing resources for AI model training and predictions. Its a win-win for both the AI and the Blockchain community.

Inception Blockchain

The Kynai Blockchain brings together miners and users who contribute GPU/CPU resources to earn the a cryptotoken. Using the cloud-agnostic tools that we provide, deep learning users are then able to train complex neural networks at scale on the blockchain.
